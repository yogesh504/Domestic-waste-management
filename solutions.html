SOLUTIONS AND AWARENESS

Interactive Workshops:

Organize workshops where residents can learn about the impacts of improper waste disposal and practical tips for responsible waste management. Digital Campaigns: Create engaging content for social media platforms, community websites, and local newsletters to disseminate information about waste management best practices.

Educational Signage:

Install informative signage near waste collection points to remind residents of proper disposal procedures and the importance of keeping the neighborhood clean.

Clean-Up Events:

Host regular community clean-up events where neighbors come together to pick up litter and beautify public spaces.
